Solid arrow (→) – Request flowing upward (e.g., “get data”, “perform computation”)

Dashed arrow (-->) – Notification flowing downward (e.g., “something changed”, “event occurred”)

Solid line without arrow (—) – Static attachment between a component and a connector (direction is implied by the arrowed lines on the same attachment)

No direct component-to-component line – Enforces C2’s rule: all communication goes through connectors
